About Us

We Aspire To Be Great!

FEGEGTA's creators have been working together for four years. We all had something on common. We aspire to be great. We have amazed the school by accomplishing great things in a short amount of time. when this semester we decided to do what we do best one last time and this time we decided to put our all since it is our last ride together as a team. so we came up with FEGEGTA (Online Psychotherapy and Mentorship System)

FEGEGTA is a platform designed to solve a problem that has been neglected by many due to various reasons but is impacting the world as we speak. The health of the human mind. This days this subject is not talked about as it should have been and many are paying the consequences because of it. FEGEGTA's goal is to fill this gap by gathering underated professionals and to provide a convenienient platform that heals Motivates energizes the human mind. The human mind is the mainframe entitiy of the this entire universe and making it work to its absolute fullest will impact the world greatly. Therfore FEGEGTA has developed an Online Psychotherapy and Mentorship system to help the society achieve at the highest level.
